About the Founder

"I believe quality assurance is not simply a department — it is a culture that must be embedded across leadership, operations, and service delivery."

I bring over 16 years of experience in the Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities (IDD) field, including more than 5 years in leadership roles within New Jersey Division of Developmental Disabilities (DDD) provider systems. My work focuses on strengthening compliance governance, operational infrastructure, and sustainable growth across community-based provider organizations.

Throughout my career, I have supported and led multi-site DDD operations with an emphasis on regulatory compliance, Medicaid and EVV governance oversight, quality assurance systems, documentation integrity, incident governance, and workforce accountability. I have worked closely with provider teams to strengthen internal oversight, improve operational consistency, and ensure services remain aligned with both regulatory expectations and participant-centered care.

Sustainable growth in the DDD space requires disciplined governance, clear performance standards, and systems that protect both the individuals served and the integrity of the organization. Supporting providers who serve individuals with developmental disabilities is deeply meaningful to me — I remain committed to strengthening ethical practices, improving compliance systems, and helping organizations operate with accountability, transparency, and service excellence.

I welcome the opportunity to work with providers committed to strengthening compliance, protecting participant rights, and building organizations that are both ethically grounded and operationally sound.
