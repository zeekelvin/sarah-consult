The Problem

NJ DDD Providers Operate
Under Intense Regulatory Pressure

Documentation That Fails to Defend Your Billing

Poor documentation is the #1 reason agencies face recoupments. Without airtight records aligned to Medicaid billing codes, your funding is at constant risk — even if the care was delivered perfectly.

Authorization & Oversight Breakdowns

Missing authorizations, expired approvals, and untracked service changes create compliance gaps that regulators exploit. Most agencies don't discover these vulnerabilities until it's too late.

Regulatory Findings & Financial Recoupments

Audit findings aren't just costly — they damage your reputation, strain operations, and put participants at risk. Building a defensible compliance system is the only sustainable protection.
