The Problem

NJ DDD Providers Operate
Under Intense Regulatory Pressure

Documentation That Fails to Defend Your Billing

Poor documentation is the #1 reason agencies face Medicaid recoupments. Without airtight service notes aligned to billing codes, ISP goals, and authorization periods, your funding is at constant risk — even if the care was delivered correctly.

Authorization & Oversight Breakdowns

Missing authorizations, expired approvals, and untracked service changes create compliance gaps that NJ DDD reviewers exploit during OPIA and fee-for-service audits. Most agencies don't discover these vulnerabilities until it's too late.

Regulatory Findings & Financial Recoupments

Audit findings aren't just costly — they damage your reputation, strain operations, and put licensing at risk. CARI background check gaps, Danielle's Law violations, and HCBS Settings Rule deficiencies can each trigger serious findings.
