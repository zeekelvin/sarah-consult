Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which service is right for me?
It depends on where your agency is in its compliance journey. If you're launching or expanding, the Startup Compliance Build gives you a full foundation. If you're already operating and want to identify gaps before an audit, the Mock Audit is ideal. Agencies with ongoing needs benefit most from our monthly Oversight retainer. If you've already received findings, Audit Rescue is your path. We're happy to discuss your situation on a free 30-minute discovery call — no commitment required.
Do you work with new providers?
Absolutely — and we strongly encourage new NJ DDD providers to engage us before they begin billing. Building your compliance infrastructure correctly from day one is far less costly than fixing gaps after an audit. The Startup Compliance Build was designed specifically for new and expanding providers who need audit-ready systems from the start. We help you launch with confidence, not guesswork.
Is this legal advice?
No. Services provided by Integrity Compliance & Consulting LLC constitute compliance consulting, not legal advice. We help agencies understand regulatory requirements, build compliant systems, and prepare documentation — but we are not attorneys. All templates, policies, and documents we produce should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel prior to implementation. Engaging our services does not create an attorney-client relationship.
